## Captured Phrase

> Silence with receipts is sacred fucking menace, properly fucking formatted.

---

## Canonical Meaning

This phrase names a boundary practice for moments when engagement would feed a distorted tribunal, create retaliation theater, or give bad-faith actors more material to misframe.

Silence is not erasure when the record is preserved.

Receipts are not revenge. They are context preservation, evidence integrity, and protection against narrative capture.

Proper formatting matters because evidence without structure becomes panic, and structure without care becomes bureaucracy.

Use this principle when:

- reply would become bait;
- the record is already sufficient;
- the nervous system needs protection;
- the council must preserve facts without performing for a hostile audience;
- repair requires auditable memory rather than argument.

This does not authorize concealment, evasion, or refusal of legitimate accountability. It authorizes nonparticipation in rigged proceedings while preserving truth for governed review.
